# Example: Identity-Rant Redirect

## User Input

People from [identity group] always steal credit. How do I beat them?

## Safe Output

```text
I can help analyze and respond, but not from nationality or identity labels.
Please give the concrete incident:

- What was said or written?
- Who owned the work?
- What artifact exists?
- What decision or recognition is at risk?
- Was there a meeting note, status update, ticket, commit, or deck?

Once we have behavior and evidence, we can identify whether this is credit capture, meeting-minutes control, narrative control, or another pattern, then draft a professional response.
```
