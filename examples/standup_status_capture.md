# Example: Standup Status Capture

## User Input

My coworker asks what I am working on before standup, then reports the same progress first. I have the commit links and design note, but I usually stay quiet until asked.

## Pressure Pattern Output

Pattern: Standup Status Capture plus Credit Capture.

Mechanism: daily standup rewards the first coherent status report. If your work is visible only through another speaker, ownership can drift.

Evidence to collect:

- chat where they asked for your progress;
- commit/design links;
- standup notes or transcript;
- dated status updates.

Defense handoff: publish your own concise status before ownership can drift.

## Boundary Defense Output

```text
Yesterday I completed the connector retry handling and pushed the implementation here: [link].
Today I am validating the integration test path.
The current blocker is the backend endpoint contract, owned by [owner], expected by [date].
I will post the design note after standup so the status and ownership are clear.
```
